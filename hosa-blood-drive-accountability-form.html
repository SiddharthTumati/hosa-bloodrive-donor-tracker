It is required for all HOSA members to either donate blood or recruit someone to donate blood. Use this form to state whether you have signed up to donate or whether you are recruiting someone (include who you will recruit).

You cannot recruit another HOSA member; they must donate or recruit on their own. You cannot recruit the same person as another member.

You may recruit MRHS teachers and students. Staff may donate in the auxiliary gym. The bus drive is full; parents and other outside guests can no longer donate.

For details, check your email, HOSA’s Instagram, the Canvas page, the March slideshow, Remind, or contact a Blood Drive Coordinator.

Members who do not donate or recruit will not be considered for officer positions.
